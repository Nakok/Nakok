### 🚁 About Me

I'm a systems tinkerer and deep RL explorer building intelligent autonomous drones that can **survive real-world network conditions**.

My research and open-source projects focus on combining:
- 🧠 **Reinforcement Learning**
- 🔁 **Real-time Network Emulation**
- ✈️ **Physics-based Simulation (AirSim)**
- 🔌 **Efficient Communication (ZeroMQ, QUIC)**

> *“If your drone can't fly with 100ms latency and 5% packet loss, is it really autonomous?”*
